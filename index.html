Four In a Row - Setting Page

(Press here for help ...)

  • Put the balls of your color in a row of 4 while the counterplayer can prevent that.
  • A point is made when you make that row horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
  • The one who get's the most points wins.
  • But first: choose the number of players in this setting page.
  • When you choose 'one', you will compete against a computer player.
  • In that case: choose a difficulty level. (for future development)
  • input player name(s) and press 'Submit Names'.
  • Press on name buttons to decide which color they get and who starts.
  • You can also decide to play full screen when you place a mark on that question.
  • To finish this page, press on 'Lets Play'.
  • Now the game begins: click on a gray cube to place a ball in that column.
  • You can play until all columns are filled, but you can also click on 'Exit' to quit.
  • In that case a rotation starts with a question if you want to end this game.
  • a blinking 'yes' or 'no' appear, you can click on this cubes to give your answer.
  • Now information is displayed who has won.
  • After a few seconds the URL line appears again when played full screen.
  • Play again? Press on 'F5' or click on the URL line and press enter.
